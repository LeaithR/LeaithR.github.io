Blood Angels Chapter


The Blood Angels chapter is a first founding chapter of the Adeptus Astartes space marines. Hailing from their homeworld of Baal located in the Ultima Segmentum. The chapter’s massive fortress monastery can be found on the desert covered surface of Baal. Serving as the Imperium’s finest warriors, the Blood Angels space marines have served and fought in countless harrowing battles throughout the galaxy. Their glorious reputation has led to many successor chapters hailing from the Blood Angels lineage including renowned chapters such as the Flesh Terrors, the Angels Resplendent, the Angels Encarmine, and many more.
